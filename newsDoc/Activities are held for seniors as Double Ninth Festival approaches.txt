Activities are held for seniors as Double Ninth Festival approaches
On this day, people pay their respects to elderly people and appreciate the wisdom they pass down. The elders celebrate the festival by enjoying autumn scenery, climbing mountains and admiring flowers like chrysanthemum, among others. /CFP
