The statue of Ingen Ryuki unveiled in Nagasaki, Japan
The statue of Ingen Ryuki was completed and unveiled on November 14 at Kofukuji Temple in Nagasaki, Japan, to celebrate the 400th anniversary of the temple. Chinese sculptor Wu Weishan created the 205-centimeter-tall bronze sculpture. Holding a 268-centimeter-long walking stick, the modest and intelligent monk presents the spirit of "helping others is helping yourself".
