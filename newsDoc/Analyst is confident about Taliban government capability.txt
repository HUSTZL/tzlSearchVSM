Analyst is confident about Taliban government capability
The UN envoy to Afghanistan says an affiliate of the militant Islamic State group has expanded, and now appears to be present in nearly all 34 provinces of the country. UN envoy Deborah Lyons says the threat of ISIS-K will grow as the economic situation deteriorates. Abdul Hai Qanit, a political analyst based in Kuala Lumpur, talks about the latest situation of Taliban fighting ISIS-K.
