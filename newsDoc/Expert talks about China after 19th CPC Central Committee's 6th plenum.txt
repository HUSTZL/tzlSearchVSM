Expert talks about China after 19th CPC Central Committee's 6th plenum
The 19th CPC Central Committee has concluded a four-day plenary session in Beijing. The committee reviewed a work report and passed a resolution on the Party's experiences and achievements. Many Western analysts are saying the sixth plenum has set the tone for the new era for China. Xia Lu, an associate professor at the School of Marxism Studies at China's Renmin University, shares his opinion.
