Rare white-cheeked gibbon born in Polish zoo
Over the past week, visitors to Wroclaw Zoo in Poland were able to see a rare sight ¨C a baby white-cheeked gibbon named Dao.

He is now one of the around 200 white-cheeked gibbons in captivity, according to the zoo.

Only 81 gibbons live in European facilities, though that number is increasing and nine have been born in the past year.
