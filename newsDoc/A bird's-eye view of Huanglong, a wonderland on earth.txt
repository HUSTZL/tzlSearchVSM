A bird's-eye view of Huanglong, a wonderland on earth
The Huanglong Scenic and Historic Interest Area in Songpan County, southwest China's Sichuan Province is a UNESCO World Heritage site. It is known for its colorful water pools, virgin forests and grand mountains. The place is also home to many animals and plants. Let's take a look at this wonderland from the sky and enjoy the beauty of mother nature.
