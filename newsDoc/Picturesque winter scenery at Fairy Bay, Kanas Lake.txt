Picturesque winter scenery at Fairy Bay, Kanas Lake
Kanas Lake is located in the north of Burqin County, northwest China's Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, spanning over an area of 45.73 square kilometers at an altitude of 1,374 meters. Fairy Bay is one of the most charming scenic spots in the Kanas Nature Reserve. It runs along a large portion of the lake and is covered with dense fog on winter mornings, creating a tranquil scene.
