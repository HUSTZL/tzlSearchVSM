Take a look at a bustling ancient town in Shanghai
Nanxiang Ancient Town in Shanghai's Jiading District is a small town with a long history. One of the four famous historical and cultural towns in Shanghai, it goes back at least 1,500 years. Today, the ancient town is a popular tourist destination in Shanghai. Check out the video to explore the bustling town.
