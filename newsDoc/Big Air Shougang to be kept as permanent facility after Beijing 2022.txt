Big Air Shougang to be kept as permanent facility after Beijing 2022
Called "Big Air Shougang," the platform is located at the former site of Capital Steel and Iron Company, also known as "Shougang," in west Beijing and is constructed on top of a decommissioned steel mill. /CFP
