Puppet Little Amal arrives in UK
Little Amal has spent the last 3 months walking 8,000 km from the border of Syria across Turkey, Greece, Italy, France, Switzerland, Germany and Belgium to the UK to raise awareness of the challenges faced by refugee children. /CFP
