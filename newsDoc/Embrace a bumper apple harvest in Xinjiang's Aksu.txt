Embrace a bumper apple harvest in Xinjiang's Aksu
Fruit-growing is a pillar industry in Aksu Prefecture, northwest China's Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region. It is a common knowledge that Aksu produces the best apples in Xinjiang. In recent years, the apple industry in the area has continued to develop through modern agricultural methods. The sweet-cored apple of Aksu is well-known across China and Southeast Asia.
