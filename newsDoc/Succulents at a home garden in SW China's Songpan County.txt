Succulents at a home garden in SW China's Songpan County
Chen Mei's garden is located in Songpan County of the Aba Tibetan and Qiang Autonomous Prefecture, southwest China's Sichuan Province. There are more than 100 kinds of plants in her garden. It's not easy to take care of these plants though. There is plenty of sunshine on the plateau, but the winter is very cold. For succulents, it's vital to keep them away from coldness and hail. In order to take care of these succulents, Chen and her family make a lot of efforts. Let's take a look at her beautiful succulents.
