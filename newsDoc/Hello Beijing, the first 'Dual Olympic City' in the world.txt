Hello Beijing, the first 'Dual Olympic City' in the world
Beijing, the capital of China, will be hosting the 2022 Winter Olympic Games in another 116 days as one of the three competition zones. Having held the Summer Olympic Games in 2008, Beijing will become the first city in the world to host both the Summer and Winter Games.

What you are watching above is only a preview of a series comprising of 12 episodes that will help you learn more about the history, culture, scenery, places of interest and Olympic venues of Beijing with three themes: Olympics, history and trending.
