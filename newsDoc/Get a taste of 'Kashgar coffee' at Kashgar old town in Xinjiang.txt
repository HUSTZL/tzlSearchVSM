Get a taste of 'Kashgar coffee' at Kashgar old town in Xinjiang
K Corner is a special coffee shop in the city Kashgar that combines local species, such as saffron, nutmeg and pomegranate, with the popular western drink.

Situated inside the old town section of the city in northwest China's Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, the coffee shop owned by Mardan Ablimit has been operating since 2017.

He created "Kashgar coffee," a special kind of coffee by blending the local herbs to the western drink. The unique blend is like a bond that connects visitors to the city's past stories.
