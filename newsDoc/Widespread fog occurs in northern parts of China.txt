Widespread fog occurs in northern parts of China
On the morning of November 3, the North China Plain witnessed widespread fog. Visible cloud images captured by the FY-4A meteorological satellite show the differences between fog and clouds; the fog does not move. Such a phenomenon is expected to occur frequently in the North China Plain until daytime on November 6.

Watch this video footage from Weather China, the China Meteorological Administration's online weather service, to find out more.
