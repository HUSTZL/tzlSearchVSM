South Africa sees upsurge in gaming during pandemic
During COVID-19 lockdowns, many of us have resorted to indoor activities. And that might just be the reason why there has been a gaming boom in the past 18 months in South Africa. With an abundance of titles on offer, gamers are spoilt for choice. CGTN's Sias du Plessis gets on the console himself, and finds out what the cool kids are playing.
