Sporting moments of the week (November 1-7)
The New York Marathon was canceled in 2020 due to coronavirus and participation in 2021 was lower than the usual 55,000 before the pandemic. Around 33,000 runners took part in the event in New York, U.S., November 7, 2021. /CFP
