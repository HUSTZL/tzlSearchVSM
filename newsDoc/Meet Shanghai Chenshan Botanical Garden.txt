Meet Shanghai Chenshan Botanical Garden
Located amidst the bustling suburbs of Shanghai, Chenshan Botanical Garden provides a green and quiet environment for the city dwellers and a center for plant science. The garden has collected more than 10,000 living taxa, emphasizing conserving plants that are significant in east China. Check out the video to see more of the garden.
