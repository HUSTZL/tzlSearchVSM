Travel back to a 1,000-year-old street in SE China's Quanzhou
If a merchant from the Song Dynasty (960-1279) would have time traveled to Quanzhou, an old port city in southeast China's Fujian Province, he would probably find every sight bizarre on a night out in the city's West Street, established 13 centuries ago. Tap the video of CGTN's "Tides of Change" series to feel the street buzz that people living hundreds of years apart could still have shared.
