Moments under U.S. Hurricane Ida
On August 29, Hurricane Ida landed in Louisiana, USA. On September 1, Hurricane Ida began to hit the northeastern United States. According to reports, at least 60 people have been killed in the hurricane in eight states in the United States. The hurricane has had a huge impact on housing, electricity and travel in the eastern United States. Please watch the moments of the hurricane under the camera of CGTN stringers.
