Visual feasts enliven streetscape of Rio de Janeiro
In the Brazilian city of Rio de Janeiro, an often-ignored side of its colorful art scene are the many pieces of graffiti and murals that are on display throughout the neighborhoods. Rio's metropolis entertains people with over 20,000 pieces of street art, painted by world-famous artists and local residents. Rio has become an ever-changing open-air gallery, and visitors are saying that it's changing the city for good.
