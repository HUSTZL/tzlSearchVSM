Henan resident shares experience of the record rainfall
At 11 a.m. on July 22, Henan's provincial meteorological observatory reissued a top-level red alert for heavy rains in Anyang, Hebi, Xinxiang, and Jiaozuo. A CGTN Stringer in Xinxiang, Henan Province, learned that near the Affiliated Middle School of Henan Normal University, the highest accumulation of water had reached waist-high levels yesterday. Now the floodwaters have begun to recede.
