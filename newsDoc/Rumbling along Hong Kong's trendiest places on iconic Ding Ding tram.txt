Rumbling along Hong Kong's trendiest places on iconic Ding Ding tram
If you've been to Hong Kong, then you're aware that it's quick and easy to get around town, whether by taxi, train or bus. But there is a more relaxing way to explore the region's main island. Hong Kong's trams have been running for over a century and have just been awarded a Guinness World Record for being the largest fleet of their kind in service. CGTN reporter Richard Kimber took a ride to find out why the tram is still as popular as ever, and to seek out the city's most mouth-watering traditional food stops along the way.
