Digital nomads make Rio de Janeiro their home
One of the great things about the digital age is the flexibility to work remotely. And this has become a lifestyle for many millennials and Gen Zers. But forgetting about working from home or your local cafes, what if you can work on a sunny strand in Rio? Well, the "Wonderful City" of Rio's tourist board has launched a dedicated website to attract young digital nomads who want to work remotely and base themselves in one of Latin America's most stunning cities. And they even have a TV channel in the favelas there. Let's take a look.
