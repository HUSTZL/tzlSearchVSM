Swans play at Bosten Lake in Xinjiang, NW China
Photos taken on October 14 show a group of whooper swans chilling out on Bosten Lake in northwest China's Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region.

Bosten, the largest inland freshwater lake stretching over 1600 square kilometers, is a major stop on the seasonal migration journey for birds.
