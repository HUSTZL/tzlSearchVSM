How Western media politicized China's performance in 2020 Olympics
Tokyo 2020 is over, with the U.S. taking top spot with 39 golds and China close behind with 38. The achievements of the two countries are something to celebrate as it shows the Olympics motto of Faster, Higher, Stronger ¨C Together. But Andy Boreham, a China-based columnist and filmmaker who comes from New Zealand, found that some Western media have published articles downplaying and politicizing China's performance as a way to cope with potential defeat.
