Protesters demand action on the climate crisis ahead of COP26
In the days heading into the UN Climate Change Summit (COP26) in Glasgow, Scotland, environmentalists around the world staged protests, demanding world leaders take real action in fighting the climate crisis and sticking to the Paris Accords 1.5-degree Celsius warming target.
