Sporting moments of the week (November 8-14)
Italy's 42-year-old rider Valentino Rossi is honored as the MotoGP Legend rider during an award ceremony in Valencia, Spain, November 14, 2021. The nine-time world champion across all categories announced his retirement from the sport after the season-ending Valencia race, which was his 432nd GP since starting his career back in 1996. /CFP
