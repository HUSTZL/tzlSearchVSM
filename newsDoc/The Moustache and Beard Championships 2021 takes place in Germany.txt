The Moustache and Beard Championships 2021 takes place in Germany
It was a facial hair extravaganza in lower Bavaria in Germany on Saturday.

The Moustache and Beard Championships 2021 took place in Pullman City Western Theme Park in Eging am See.

Close to 100 participants competed against each other in categories broken down into mustaches, partial beards, full beards and craft. Take a look at some of the contenders.
