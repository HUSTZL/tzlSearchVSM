China's Wing Loong-2 Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) in 60 seconds
China's latest Wing Loong-2 Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) completed the first large UAV flight demonstration at the Air Show China 2021. It showed the capabilities of autonomous taking-off and landing, climb and air-ground coordination. Wing Loong-2 is a multi-role UAV independently designed and produced by the Aviation Industry Corporation of China. It is equipped with electronic reconnaissance and communication equipment that can obtain and send back high-resolution images on time. It can also carry small air-to-ground precision attack weapons. Wing Loong-2 is able to conduct both reconnaissance and strike missions.
