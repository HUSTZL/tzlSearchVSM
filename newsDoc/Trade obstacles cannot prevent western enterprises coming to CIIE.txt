Trade obstacles cannot prevent western enterprises coming to CIIE
The 4th China International Import Expo underway in Shanghai is larger in scale and boasts more exhibitors than before. Despite the headwind of economic and trade friction, the enthusiasm of European and American enterprises to participate in the expo is even stronger, which shows that the world economy is deeply integrated and the general trend of cooperation is irreversible. In this episode of BizBeat, CGTN's Zhu Zhu explains.
