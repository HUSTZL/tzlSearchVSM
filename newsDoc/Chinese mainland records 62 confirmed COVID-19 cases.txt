Chinese mainland records 62 confirmed COVID-19 cases
The Chinese mainland recorded 62 newly-confirmed COVID-19 cases on Wednesday, with 47 linked to local transmissions and 15 from overseas, data from the National Health Commission showed on Thursday.



In addition, 35 new asymptomatic cases were recorded, while 538 asymptomatic patients remain under medical observation.



Confirmed cases on the Chinese mainland now total 98,001, with the death toll of 4,636 unchanged since January.



The latest tally of confirmed cases in the Hong Kong and Macao special administrative regions and Taiwan region is as follows:



Hong Kong: 12,374 (12,036 recoveries, 213 deaths)



Macao: 77 (75 recoveries)



Taiwan: 16,456 (13,742 recoveries, 848 deaths)
