CCCC holds open house event for Mombasa-Nairobi railway in Kenya
China Communications Construction Company, Ltd. (CCCC) recently held an open house event for the Mombasa-Nairobi Standard Gauge Railway in Kenya, showcasing operation and maintenance of the railway and how it has changed local people's lives.

The largest infrastructure project in Kenya since its independence in 1963, the railway is a flagship project of Kenya Vision 2030 ¨C the long-term development blueprint for the country ¨C and an early outcome of China's Belt and Road Initiative.
