Birds captured on camera at Ulungur Lake National Wetland Park (III)
Various types of birds were captured on camera by Aymurat Dauletbek, a park staff at the Ulungur Lake National Wetland Park in Fuhai County, northwest China's Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region. The lake, the largest permanent freshwater lake in northern Xinjiang, is well known for its fabulous natural scenery. Covering 827 square kilometers in area, it is an ideal home for migratory birds.
