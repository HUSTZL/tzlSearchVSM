COP15 opens in SW China's Kunming
Media center of the 15th meeting of the Conference of the Parties to the UN Convention on Biological Diversity (COP15) at the Kunming Dianchi International Convention and Exhibition Center on October 11, 2021. Opening from 8 a.m. to 9 p.m. between October 9 and 15, the center has two press conference halls that can accommodate 140 people and 100 people respectively. Participating media outlets can also go online to conduct interviews. /CFP
